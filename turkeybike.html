Turkey Bike Tour


This map was created for a "Graphic Design in Cartography" course. The assignment was to produce a terrain map highlight a 21-day bike tour anywhere in the world. I chose Turkey because I lived there, and it was fun to route the tour across two continents and along three seas! You probably can't say that about most countries. Fonts used are Futura and Palotino.
