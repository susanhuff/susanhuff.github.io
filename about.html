HELLO

I recently relocated to Seattle from Washington, D.C. where I made thematic maps for the U.S. Government. I'm excited to learn a new city and have my husband, who is from the Pacific Northwest, be my tour guide for years to come. I knew I loved geography and maps since I was eight when I received my first puzzle of the United States. Learning about different places, cultures, and people was always intriguing to me. I guess that's why I love to travel too!

Making maps is one way I can tell people's stories of where and how they live. Find my resume here to learn more about me.
