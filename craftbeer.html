U.S. CRAFT BEER INDUSTRY

This infographic was created for a "Graphic Design in Cartography" course. Topic chosen for fun and it was easier to find the data. Living in D.C. since 2003, I saw the rise of craft beer (no more Coors Light for me!) in the area and wondered about the history and geography of the industry. Color palette was influenced by the color of beer and its ingredients (i.e. barley and hops). Fonts used are Futura, Copperplate Gothic, and Bourbon.
